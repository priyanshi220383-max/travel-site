Pilgrimage & Trekking
Hemkunt Sahib
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Nature & Trekking Rating

Hemkund Sahib is one of the highest and most revered Sikh pilgrimage sites in the world, located at an altitude of over 4,300 meters in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand. Surrounded by seven snow-capped peaks and a pristine glacial lake, the gurudwara attracts thousands of devotees and trekkers every year. The journey to Hemkund Sahib offers a unique combination of spirituality, adventure and breathtaking natural beauty.

State Uttarakhand
Best Time June - September
Altitude 4329 m
Famous For Hemkund Sahib Gurudwara
Trip Duration 2 - 3 Days
Nearest Railway Station Rishikesh
Nearest Airport Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun

Featured Guides

Places To Visit

Visit Hemkund Sahib Gurudwara, Hemkund Lake, Valley of Flowers, Ghangaria and nearby Himalayan trails.

Activities To Do

Enjoy trekking, nature photography, spiritual journeys, mountain exploration and visiting alpine meadows.

Food Guide

Relish simple vegetarian meals, langar at the gurudwara and traditional mountain cuisine during the trek.

Shopping Guide

Shop for religious souvenirs, prayer items, woollen clothing and trekking essentials in nearby towns.

Scenic View Points

Witness spectacular views of glacial lakes, snow-covered peaks, alpine valleys and Himalayan landscapes.
