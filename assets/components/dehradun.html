Dehradun, the capital city of Uttarakhand, is a perfect blend of natural beauty, modern attractions and rich heritage. Nestled in the Doon Valley between the Himalayas and Shivalik ranges, the city is famous for its pleasant climate, caves, waterfalls, temples, educational institutions and vibrant cafés. It serves as the gateway to popular destinations like Mussoorie, Rishikesh and the Char Dham pilgrimage circuit.
