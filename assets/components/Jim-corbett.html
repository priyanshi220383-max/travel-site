Jim Corbett National Park
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Nature & Trekking Rating

Jim Corbett National Park is one of the most popular national parks in India, known for its rich biodiversity and tiger population. The park offers a unique blend of wildlife sanctuaries, dense forests, and scenic landscapes that attract nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts from around the world.

State Uttarakhand
Best Time November - June
Altitude 385 m
Famous For Tiger Safaris & Wildlife
Trip Duration 2 - 4 Days
Nearest Railway Station Ramnagar
Nearest Airport Pantnagar Airport
