Rishikesh, known as the Yoga Capital of the World, is a spiritual and adventure hub nestled along the banks of the sacred Ganga River. Famous for yoga retreats, river rafting, suspension bridges, temples, cafés and Himalayan views, Rishikesh attracts travelers seeking both peace and adrenaline-filled experiences.
