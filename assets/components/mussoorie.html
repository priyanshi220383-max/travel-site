Hill Station
Mussoorie
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Tourist Rating

Popularly known as the Queen of Hills, Mussoorie is one of India's most loved hill stations. Surrounded by the Garhwal Himalayas, it offers breathtaking mountain views, colonial charm, scenic waterfalls, bustling markets, cozy cafés and exciting adventure activities. Its pleasant weather and stunning landscapes make it a perfect getaway for families, couples and nature lovers.

State Uttarakhand
Best Time March - June & September - November
Altitude 2005 m
Famous For Kempty Falls & Mall Road
Trip Duration 2 - 3 Days
Nearest Railway Station Dehradun
Nearest Airport Jolly Grant Airport

Enjoy ropeway rides, trekking, ziplining, mountain walks, camping and photography adventures.

Discover charming cafés, bakeries, local eateries and mountain-view restaurants across Mussoorie.
