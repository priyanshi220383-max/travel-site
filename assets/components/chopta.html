Mini Switzerland of Uttarakhand
Chopta
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Nature & Trekking Rating

Chopta is a serene hill station often called the "Mini Switzerland of Uttarakhand." Surrounded by dense forests, lush meadows and stunning Himalayan peaks, Chopta is a paradise for trekkers, campers and nature lovers. It serves as the base for the famous Tungnath Temple and Chandrashila Trek, offering breathtaking sunrise views and unforgettable mountain experiences.

State Uttarakhand
Best Time March - June & September - November
Altitude 2680 m
Famous For Tungnath Temple & Chandrashila Trek
Trip Duration 2 - 3 Days
Nearest Railway Station Rishikesh
Nearest Airport Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun

Featured Guides

Places To Visit

Visit Tungnath Temple, Chandrashila Summit, Deoria Tal, Ukhimath and Kedarnath Wildlife Sanctuary.

Activities To Do

Enjoy trekking, camping, bird watching, photography, stargazing and nature walks through alpine meadows.

Food Guide

Taste authentic Garhwali cuisine, local delicacies, mountain tea and homestyle meals at camps and cafés.

Shopping Guide

Buy handmade woollens, local handicrafts, souvenirs and traditional Himalayan products from nearby markets.

Scenic View Points

Witness panoramic views of Nanda Devi, Trishul, Chaukhamba and the snow-covered Himalayan ranges.
