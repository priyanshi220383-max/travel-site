Pilgrimage Destination
Badrinath
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Tourist Rating

Badrinath is one of the most revered pilgrimage destinations in India and a key part of the Char Dham Yatra. Situated on the banks of the Alaknanda River amidst the majestic Himalayas, Badrinath is famous for the sacred Badrinath Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Surrounded by snow-covered peaks, hot springs and breathtaking landscapes, it attracts devotees and nature lovers from across the world.

StateUttarakhand
Best TimeMay – June & September – October
Altitude3,133 m
Famous ForBadrinath Temple, Char Dham
Nearest RailwayRishikesh
Nearest AirportJolly Grant Airport, Dehradun
