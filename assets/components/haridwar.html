Haridwar
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Tourist Rating

Haridwar is one of the holiest cities in India, located on the banks of the sacred Ganga River. Known for its spiritual atmosphere, ancient temples, mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri and vibrant markets, Haridwar attracts pilgrims and travelers from around the world seeking culture, devotion and tranquility.

State Uttarakhand
Best Time October - April
Altitude 314 m
Famous For Har Ki Pauri & Ganga Aarti
Trip Duration 1 - 2 Days
Nearest Railway Station Haridwar Junction
Nearest Airport Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun

Featured Guides

Places To Visit

Explore Har Ki Pauri, Mansa Devi Temple, Chandi Devi Temple, Bharat Mata Mandir and Shantikunj Ashram.

Activities To Do

Attend the famous Ganga Aarti, take a holy dip in the Ganges, enjoy ropeway rides and explore spiritual sites.

Food Guide

Taste authentic North Indian cuisine, street food, sweets, kachoris, chaat and traditional vegetarian dishes.

Shopping Guide

Shop for religious items, Rudraksha beads, handicrafts, Ayurvedic products, souvenirs and local sweets.

Scenic View Points

Enjoy beautiful views of the Ganga River, temple-covered hills, evening aartis and panoramic city landscapes.
