Hill Station
Auli
⭐ 4.8 / 5 Tourist Rating

Auli is a breathtaking hill station and ski destination located in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand. Surrounded by snow-capped Himalayan peaks, dense forests and scenic meadows, Auli is famous for skiing, cable car rides, trekking and panoramic mountain views. It is one of India's top destinations for adventure lovers and nature enthusiasts seeking a peaceful escape.

State Uttarakhand
Best Time December - March (Snow) & April - June
Altitude 2800 m
Famous For Skiing & Himalayan Views
Trip Duration 2 - 4 Days
Nearest Railway Station Rishikesh
Nearest Airport Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun

Featured Guides

Places To Visit

Visit Auli Artificial Lake, Gurso Bugyal, Chattrakund Lake, Joshimath and Narsingh Temple.

Activities To Do

Enjoy skiing, snowboarding, trekking, camping, cable car rides and mountain photography.

Food Guide

Savor local Garhwali cuisine, mountain cafés, traditional dishes and warm beverages with scenic views.

Shopping Guide

Shop for woollen clothes, handmade crafts, local souvenirs and traditional Himalayan products.

Scenic View Points

Witness spectacular views of Nanda Devi, Kamet, Mana Parvat and the snow-covered Himalayan ranges.
